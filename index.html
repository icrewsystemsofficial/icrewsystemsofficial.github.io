Feed the Poor

There are always people who strive to help. There are people who deep down "want" to help, but they're haunted by one burning question, 'will my money actually being used to feed the poor?'. This is what we are solving!.

#feedThePoor initiative is all about Transparency. With the help of our NGO partners, we are able to show our donors who they've fed, and how every penny of their donation is putting a smile across thousands of faces.
